# AdNet Services Overview
Yardstick ONDC AdNet comprises a suite of services built on top of the Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC) protocol. These services are designed to enhance the advertising and commerce ecosystem within the ONDC network.



## AdNet Services Overview

AdNet Services consist of three main components:

1. **Seller AdNet App**: Tailored for ONDC Seller Apps, this component enables sellers to:
   - Create and manage advertising campaigns from their Seller Inventory
   - Target specific audiences with keywords
   - Track Ad performance over time

<br />

2. **Buyer AdNet App**: Designed for ONDC Buyer Apps, this component allows buyers to:
   - Create and manage Ad Units
   - Integrate these Ad Units into their applications
   - Select from a variety of Ad Formats: Search, Display and Menu 

<br />


3. **Ad Auction Service**: Core Service that handles Ad Delivery from Seller Inventory to the Buyer App. It performs:
    - Real Time Bidding of Ads based on Buyer Context and Ad Unit
    - Tracking of Ad Impressions and Clicks
    - Budget Management for Sellers
